Dr. Chao Zhou is a Principal Researcher at Tencent Advertising, where he brings over 13 years of leadership in R&D and technical management across Recommendation Systems, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Artificial Intelligence. He received his PhD degree from <a href='https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/english/index.html'>The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)</a> under the supervision of <a href='https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/people/faculty/irwin-king/'>Prof. Irwin King</a> and <a href='https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/lyu/home'>Prof. Michael R. Lyu</a>. He received his Bachelor degree from <a href='http://ckc.zju.edu.cn/ckcen/'>Chu KoChen Honors College</a> <a href='https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/'>Zhejiang University</a> under the supervision of <a href='http://talent.zju.edu.cn/2023/0112/c72923a2709237/page.htm'>Prof. Chun Chen</a> and <a href='https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/bjj'>Prof. Jiajun Bu</a>.

Dr. Zhou has published 15 papers in premier venues such as AAAI, SIGIR, TOIS, CIKM, VLDB, MM, and ACL, and holds 65 patents in the US and China. His core technical achievements include spearheading the ultra-large-scale expansion of advertising recommendation models, pioneering LLM-based generative retrieval, and leading the development of the Tencent Cloud AI platform. His work spans speech and semantic technologies as well as the industrial implementation of diverse AI-to-B solutions. Beyond his technical roles, he serves as an External Mentor at CUHK-Shenzhen and was a key organizer for the 2025 Tencent Advertising Algorithm Challenge.
